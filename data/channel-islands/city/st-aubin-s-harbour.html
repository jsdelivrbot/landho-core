St Aubin's Harbour - Profile

Location

A sheltered drying harbour at the head of St Aubin's Bay, just west of St Helier.

Position 49° 11'14"N, 02° 10'00"W.

Docking

Visiting yachts should only enter one hour either side of high water and moor up on the wall immediately to starboard.

Facilities include water, fuel and some repair facilities.
