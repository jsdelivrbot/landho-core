Gran Tarajal - Profile

Location

Gran Tarajal lies four miles SW of Punta Lantailla on the South East coast of Fuerteventura. This attractive town is surrounded by hills on three sides giving shelter from the almost constant trade winds.

Position 28° 12.5'N, 14° 01.7'W

Docking

The harbour has good protection from the south and west with two pontoons in the western part.

The new marina here provides berths for yachtsmen and the local fishing fleet as well as a centre for the new sailing school. There is reported to be plenty of space with the pontoons to port being for yachts over 15m. It is also very reasonable for overnight berthing.

Arriving yachts should come alongside the main quay and report to the port office by the main harbour gates (open 0800-1400 Monday-Friday).
