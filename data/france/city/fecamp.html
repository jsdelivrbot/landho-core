Fecamp - Profile

Docking

This ancient fishing port is easily entered by day or night and once inside the harbour, the marina is immediately to sta'bd in the Avant Port. Berthing anywhere on pontoon C. If staying longer than a couple of days, then locking through to Bassin Berigny would offer a more comfortable berth in strong SW'ly winds. The lock operates HW-0230 to HW+0045mins.

Weather forecast on a screen outside the capitainerie, updated every three hours

The etrance is now at chart datum (2007). Iit is dredged every few years to 1.5m. The shallowest spot is just inside the entrance. Anchoring outside is only advisable in the calmest of conditions.

Port has 600 berths of which 70 are for visitors. Access to the outer (sea) basin is possible at all states of the tide but check minimum draft. Fuel dock,water and electricity on all berths, toilets, showers and launderette, WiFi. 38 ton crane, scrubbing grid.
